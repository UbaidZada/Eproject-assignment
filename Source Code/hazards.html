Weather

Mountain weather can be unpredictable and change rapidly, even during the same day. Here are some tips to help you prepare for mountain weather conditions. Temperatures can very widely in the mountains, so it's important to dress in layers. Wear a moisture-wicking base layers, a insulting mid-layer,and a waterproof outer layer to protect against rain, snow, and wind. In addition to clothing, bring appropriate gear such as gloves, hats, and sunscreen. if you're camping, make sure you have a sturdy tent and sleeping bag rated for the expected Temperatures. The weather in the mountains can change quickly, so always be prepared and flexible. stay safe and enjoy your outdoor adventure.
